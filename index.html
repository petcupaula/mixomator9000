Cleaning Procedure

1. Prepare a cleaning container with water (maybe even hot water, or with bleach)

2. Take all tubes out of the containers and put them into the cleaning container

3. Put a cup into the machine to collect the water with drink residues from the tubes

4. Order the CLEAN procedure (maybe even several times until the water comes out clean)

5. Take all the tubes out of the cleaning container

6. Order the CLEAN procedure one more time, in order to move the water through the tubes and fill it with air instead so that we don't end up with storing water in the tubes
